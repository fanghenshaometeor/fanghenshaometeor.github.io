I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Institute of Image Processing and Pattern Recognition at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My advisor is Prof. Jie Yang. Before coming to SJTU, I received the BA.Eng. degree in Department of Control Science and Engineering from Tongji University in 2018. I then received the MA.Eng. degree in Department of Automation from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021.

research

My research direction focuses on deep learning and pattern recognition, mainly on adversarial robustness and kernel methods. Besides, I have been participating in two projects as an intern in Medtronic, medical image denoising and 3d reconstruction of endoscope image.
